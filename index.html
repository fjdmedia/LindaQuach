About

Hi, I'm Linda.

I'm a Winnipeg-based photographer who believes the best photos happen when you forget the camera is there. I focus on the in-between moments — the laugh before the kiss, the look across the room, the quiet pause that says everything.

My style is warm, editorial, and real. No stiff posing, no over-editing. Just you two, your story, and golden hour light. Whether it's an engagement session, a wedding day, or a family milestone — I'm here to document what matters most.

The quiet moments are the ones worth keeping.
