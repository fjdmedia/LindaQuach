About Me.

Hi, I'm Linda — a photographer based in Winnipeg, MB with a love for capturing real, unscripted moments. I believe the most beautiful photos aren't posed — they're felt.

From proposals on the beach to snow-covered engagements in the city, I show up with my camera and a whole lot of heart. My style blends documentary and editorial — quietly observant, but always intentional.

Every love story deserves to be told beautifully. Let's tell yours.
